   1.  maps elements of $X$ into real numbers.
   2. a scalar measure of undesiability of choosing decision $x$.

2. Constraint set $X$ : consists of available decisions $x$.

   1. Decision $ x$: n tuple of real numbers $(x_1,\ldots,x_n)$.
